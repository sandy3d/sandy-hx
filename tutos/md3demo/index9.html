SANDY DOCS

Controls:
w,a,d,x: Move forward, left, right, backwards
e,c: Move upwards, downwards
<shift>: 'Run'
s: look at origin (0,0,0)
1,2,3,4: different starting points
Mouse: tilt/pan camera
Mouse click: freeze/unfreeze mouse tilt and pan
m: toggle wireframe material for ground
i: create new model instance
+/-: change controlled object (ground and origin axis will not move)
h: toggle mouse move handler
